Projects and Work Experience

Detecting Gender Bias in Physician Comments - Stanford InfoLab Research

Developed an analysis to detect gender bias in patient comments about their physicians. Ran a logistic regression on the presence of words associated with compassion, ambition, and attitude on a database of 125,000 comments and quantitative ratings, controlling for possible confounding variablels such as physician speciality, race, and age.

Interleaving Deep Dive: Data visualization for Bing Feature Experiments - Microsoft AI + Research Internship

Built an end-to-end pipeline for developers at Bing to better understand the results of feature experiments. Developed generalizable scripts to mine data from Microsoft servers, filter significantly moved queries, and parse the ranker data to reveal the original and new rankers for those queries.

You can check out my final presentation (with a demo of my work) here.
